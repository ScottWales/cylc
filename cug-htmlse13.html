A Suite.rc Reference

This appendix documents all legal entries in a suite.rc file. Most suites will only need to explicitly configure a few of these items - many of them have sensible default values, some may only be needed for critical operational suites (e.g. secure passphrases), and some are primarily used for cylc development. In general your suite.rc files shouldn’t be a lot more complicated than those of the cylc example suites.

See also Suite Definition - Suite.rc Overview (Section 8.2) for a descriptive overview of suite.rc files.
