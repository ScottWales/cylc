H Known Bugs At Version 3.5.1

  • Conditional triggers containing both AND and OR in the same expression work properly at run time, but they are not graphed correctly.
  • Sometimes the gcylc database view does not update when a suite or group has been unregistered by another process. Workaround: restart gcylc.
