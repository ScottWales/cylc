5.5 Plotting The examples:CUG_QS1 Dependency Graph

Right-click on the examples:CUG_QS1 suite in gcylc and choose Graph; or on the command line,

$ cylc graph examples:CUG_QS1 2011052300 2011052318 &

This should pop up a zoomable, pannable, graph viewer showing the graph of Figure 20.

If you edit the suite.rc file while viewing the graph, the viewer will update whenever you save the file.6
